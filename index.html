About Bloomhaven Care

Our Mission & Vision

At Bloomhaven Care, we believe that quality healthcare should be accessible, compassionate, and delivered with dignity. We have revolutionized home healthcare in Nairobi by bringing professional, qualified nurses and caregivers directly to your doorstep.

Our team of experienced African nurses understands the cultural nuances and personal needs of our community. We combine medical expertise with genuine empathy to provide healthcare that feels like family care.

Whether you need post-operative support, maternal care, elderly companionship, or chronic disease management, Bloomhaven Care is your trusted partner in wellness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a consultation? +

Booking a consultation is easy! Simply fill out our contact form on this page, call us at +254 705 790 425, or send us a message via WhatsApp. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours to discuss your healthcare needs and create a customized care plan.

Are your nurses qualified and licensed? +

Yes! All our nurses are fully qualified, licensed, and registered with the Kenya Nursing Council. They undergo continuous training and are background-checked. Your safety and quality care are our top priorities.

What areas do you serve? +

We currently serve Nairobi and surrounding areas within a 30km radius. This includes suburbs such as Karen, Westlands, Kilimani, Langata, and others. Contact us to confirm if your location is within our service area.

What are your pricing options? +

Our pricing is transparent and flexible. We offer hourly rates, daily packages, and monthly care plans. Rates vary based on the type of care needed. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your specific requirements.

Do you offer 24/7 emergency support? +

Absolutely! Our 24/7 support line is always available for emergencies, urgent consultations, and questions. Call +254 705 790 425 or +254 717 715 039 at any time, day or night. We're here for you.

How do you ensure patient privacy? +

Patient privacy and confidentiality are sacred to us. We follow all healthcare regulations and data protection laws. Your medical information is secure, encrypted, and only shared with authorized healthcare providers as needed for your care.
